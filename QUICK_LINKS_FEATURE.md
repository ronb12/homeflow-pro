## 📝 How To Use:

### **Adding a Quick Link:**

1. Go to Quick Links in sidebar
2. Click "Add New"
3. Fill in:
   ```
   Link Name: Electric Company
   URL: https://www.myelectricco.com
   Category: Utilities
   Description: Account #12345
   ```
4. Click "Add"
5. Link appears with 🔗 icon - **click to open!**
